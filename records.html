News and Updates

Free Flu Shots and Wellness Check-Ups

Date: September 5, 2024

Campus Health Clinic Announces Free Flu Shots and Wellness Check-Ups Read More

We’re thrilled to share that starting Monday, September 11th, the Campus Health Clinic will be offering free flu shots to all students. Our goal is to help keep everyone healthy as flu season kicks in. You can stop by anytime from 9 AM to 5 PM, Monday through Friday, for a quick and easy walk-in appointment. But that’s not all—we’re also providing free wellness check-ups and sharing tips on how to stay healthy as the weather cools down. Make sure to bring your student ID, and feel free to ask us any questions about staying well this school year. For more info, check out our website or swing by the front desk. We’re here to help you stay healthy and happy!

Mental Health Support

Date: August 30, 2024

New Mental Health Support at the Campus Health Clinic!Read More

We’re thrilled to share that starting Monday, September 11th, the Campus Health Clinic is expanding our mental health services just for you. We know that college life can be overwhelming, and we want to make sure you have the support you need. We’re now offering one-on-one counseling sessions, group therapy, and stress-relief workshops to help you manage stress and stay balanced. You can easily book an appointment or join a workshop by visiting us at the clinic or checking out our website. Don’t forget to bring your student ID, and let us know if you have any questions or need additional support. We’re here to help you feel your best and make this school year a little easier. For more info, swing by the front desk or visit our website. We’re excited to support your mental well-being!
