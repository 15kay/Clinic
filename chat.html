Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What services are offered at the campus clinic?

The campus clinic offers a range of services including general health check-ups, emergency care, vaccinations, mental health counseling, and medical referrals. For a detailed list, please visit our Services page or contact the clinic directly.

How can I book an appointment?

Appointments can be booked online through our Appointment page

What should I bring to my appointment?

For your appointment, please bring a valid student ID, any relevant medical records, and your insurance information if applicable. It is also helpful to arrive a few minutes early to complete any necessary paperwork.

Is there a cost for clinic services?

Most services at the campus clinic are covered by student fees. However, some specialized treatments or medications may incur additional costs. Please check with the clinic's billing office for detailed information on costs and coverage.

How can I contact the clinic if I have more questions?

You can contact the campus clinic by phone or email. Our contact information is listed on the Contact Us page of our website. We are also available for in-person inquiries during regular office hours.
